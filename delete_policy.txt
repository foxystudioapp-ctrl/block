Block Blast Account Deletion

To request the deletion of your Block Blast (com.askar.blockblast) account and all associated data, you have two options:

Option 1: In-App Deletion
1. Open the Block Blast app.
2. Navigate to the Profile / Settings screen.
3. Click the 'Delete Account' button to immediately and permanently delete your account, email address, and all saved scores from our Firebase servers.

Option 2: Email Request
You can also request account deletion by emailing the developer.

Data Deleted: Your Google account association, username, authentication records, and all game progress/scores.
Data Retained: None. All personal data is completely removed upon deletion.
