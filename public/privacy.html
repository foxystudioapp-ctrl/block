1. Information Collection & Cloud Sync

Bloxy uses Google Sign-In (Gmail) to securely authenticate you and automatically synchronize your game progress, diamonds, high scores, and unlocked items to our Firebase cloud servers. This allows you to recover your progress when you log into a new device. By logging in, you consent to storing this game data on our secure cloud database.

2. Use of Information

Any data stored locally is used solely to provide a seamless gameplay experience. We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties.

3. Permissions

The app may request access to internet connectivity for leaderboard updates, daily tasks, and multiplayer mode synchronization. No background tracking or location data is collected.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at foxystudioapp@gmail.com.
