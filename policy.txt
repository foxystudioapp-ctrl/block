Privacy Policy for Bloxy

This privacy policy applies to the Bloxy app (package: com.askar.blockblast).

1. Data Collection
We use Firebase Authentication and Google Sign-In to save your game scores, diamonds, and overall progress to the cloud. This provides a seamless way to recover your data across devices.

Your data is encrypted and securely stored by Google Firebase. We do not sell or share your data with any third parties.
